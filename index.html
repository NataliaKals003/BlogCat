Would the cats be astronauts?

Cats are fascinating creatures surrounded by mysteries. With independent and mysterious temperaments, these felines are known for their agility, elegance, and often unpredictable behavior. However, recently, a theory has gained traction on the internet and among conspiracy theorists: could cats be astronauts?

The theory suggests that cats are not terrestrial animals, but rather extraterrestrial beings sent to carry out a specific mission on our planet. There are historical accounts that point to the fact that cats were considered sacred beings in different cultures and even associated with divine figures. This could indicate that cats have a much more mystical origin than previously thought.

Furthermore, there are theorists who believe that felines possess telepathic abilities and are capable of communicating with other alien species. This belief is based on reports from people who claim to have seen cats reacting to stimuli that are not perceptible to humans, or even behaving unusually during astronomicalevents such as the Northern Lights. These and other clues have been interpreted as signs that cats could be interplanetary beings.
